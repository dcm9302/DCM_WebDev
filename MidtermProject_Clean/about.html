ABOUT

Hi! I'm Daniela Marin, a student at NYU majoring in Integrated Design and Media with a minor in Psychology. Creativity has always been at the core of who I am--whether through drawing, crocheting, baking, or photography. I never knew how to channel my passion for visual storytelling into a career until I started exploring marketing strategy, advertising, UX/UI Design, and graphic design.

My goal is to create meaningful experiences and visuals that connect people with brands and ideas. I'm passionate about designing intuitive user experiences, creating impactful marketing strategies, and building user-friendly apps and visuals that drive meaningful initiatives. I'm constantly learning and evolving, and this website serves as a glimpse into my journey as a designer.
