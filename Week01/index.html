Chai Spice Cake Recipe

A fluffy chai spice cake with lavender filling and salted chocolate buttercream frosting.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Unsalted butter, softened
  • Milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Bake the cake with chai spices
  2. Prepare the lavender filling
  3. Make the salted chocolate buttercream
  4. Assemble and decorate
